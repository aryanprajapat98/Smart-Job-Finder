1. Introduction

Welcome to Smart Job Finder. Hum aapki privacy ko bahut seriously lete hain. Yeh Privacy Policy batati hai ki hum kaunse information collect karte hain, kaise use karte hain, aur kaise protect karte hain jab aap hamari website ka use karte ho.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Naam, Email, Phone Number, Resume, Education details, Experience jab aap account banate ho ya job apply karte ho.
  • Usage Data: IP Address, Browser type, Device information, Pages visited.
  • Job Search Data: Search keywords, Applied jobs, Saved jobs.
  • Cookies & Tracking: Better user experience ke liye.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Job recommendations dene ke liye
  • Job applications process karne ke liye
  • Account management aur support dene ke liye
  • Website ko improve aur personalize karne ke liye
  • Important updates aur job alerts bhejne ke liye

4. Information Sharing

Hum aapki personal information kisi teesri party ke saath nahi share karte, jab tak:

  • Aap job apply karte ho (tab sirf company ko necessary details jaate hain)
  • Legal requirements ho
  • Service providers (hosting, email service) ke saath (wo bhi strict privacy ke under)

5. Data Security

Hum aapke data ko secure rakhne ke liye industry standard security measures use karte hain. Lekin koi bhi online platform 100% secure nahi hota.

6. Your Rights

  • Aap apna data access, update, ya delete karwa sakte ho
  • Job alerts unsubscribe kar sakte ho
  • Account delete karne ka request kar sakte ho

7. Cookies Policy

Hum cookies ka use aapko better experience dene ke liye karte hain. Aap apne browser settings se cookies manage kar sakte hain.

8. Changes in Privacy Policy

Hum is policy ko time-to-time update kar sakte hain. Har update ke baad yeh page update ho jaayega.
